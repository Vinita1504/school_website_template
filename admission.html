Admission

Admission is open to classes pre-Nursery to class VIII at XYX School Patna. The school aspires to produce students who are excellent in academics as well as social and ethical values, skills and attitudes to contribute positively to the society. Thus, the admission process Is designed to test both aptitude and attitude, While the test assess the proficiency in various subjects, it will also reveal the child’s problem-solving skills. Portions integrated In the admission process will also examine the applicant’s belief and value system on the basis of which he/she makes decisions and the way he/she handles and negotiates the external world. We believe that this method of testing the Student will allow us to look at him/her holistically.



  • Registration is the first step fo seeking admission at XYX School.
  • Registration form will be available on all working days (Monday-Saturday).
  • Admission to Montessory section will be through informal interaction with Parents and child.
  • Incomplete Registration form will not be processed.
  • Parents are advised not to fill more than one form for an applicant.

Registration

The Prospectus and Admission Information booklet is available at our school office. The parents are requested to fill-up the registration form very carefully. It is advised that parents should fill the Registration Form themselves to avoid any incomplete information. The applications will be scrutinized and only those found complete in all respects will be selected for Observation/Assessment Test. Applying for registration does not imply admission. It is subject to the availability of seats.
